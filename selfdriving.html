Self-Driving Cars

One particular application of computing that is making positive contibutions to society is self-driving cars.

One example of a company that is developing self driving cars is Waymo.

The technology used in self-driving cars is designed to detect objects and hazards such as pedestrians, cyslists, vehicles, and road works.

Here are some facts about road traffic accidents:

  • In 2014 there were 1.25 million deaths worldwide due to vehicle crashes.
  • There were 32,675 deaths in the United States due to vehicle crashes in 2014.
  • In 2016, there was a 6% increase in traffic fatalities, reaching the highest point in nearly a decade.
